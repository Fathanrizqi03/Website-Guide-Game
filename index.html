Welcome to the Survivor.io Beginner's Guide

If you’re a big fan of roguelike adventure games then you have probably found Survivor.io by now. It is tonnes of fun and, even better, it is available to be played on mobile when you are on the go. However, things get a little hairy when you first start as the hordes of enemies just keep coming. Read this Survivor.io Guide for all the Best Tips and Tricks for Beginners.

Getting Started

Step 1: Choose Your Gear

Before starting a run, equip your character with the best weapons, armor, and accessories available. Early on, focus on:

  • Weapons: The Lightchaser or Kunai are excellent beginner choices.
  • Armor: Choose armor that boosts HP and provides crowd control benefits.
  • Accessories: Equip items that enhance healing, movement speed, or attack power.

Step 2: Start Small

Begin with the easier chapters to learn enemy patterns and practice dodging. Progress to harder stages as you improve.

Surviving the Waves

Movement Tips:

  • Always stay mobile to avoid getting surrounded by enemies.
  • Lead enemies into groups to maximize the effectiveness of area-of-effect (AoE) skills.

Skill Selection:

When leveling up, you’ll be presented with random skill options. Prioritize these:

  • Primary Weapon Evolution: Focus on evolving your main weapon by collecting its compatible passive skill.
  • Example: Kunai evolves into the Spirit Shuriken when paired with the [Energy Cube].
  • AoE Damage Skills: Skills like Molotov and Lightning Emitter are great for crowd control.
  • Survivability Skills: Abilities like Forcefield or Healing Drone can save you in tough situations.

Upgrading and Evolution

Upgrading Skills:

Combine passive skills with active ones for powerful evolutions. For example:

  • Guardian + Fitness Guide = Defender.
  • Lightning Emitter + Energy Cube = Thunderbolt Power Core.

Weapon Evolution:

Upgrade your weapon to its max level during a run to unlock its evolution.

Tips for Success

Here are some tips to help you succeed in Survivor.io:

  • Always keep moving to avoid getting surrounded by enemies.
  • Prioritize upgrading your primary weapon for maximum damage output.
  • Use your special abilities wisely and save them for tough situations.
  • Collect health packs and other resources whenever you can.
  • Learn the attack patterns of different enemies to dodge their attacks effectively.

Daily Rewards and Events

Survivor.io offers daily rewards and special events that can give you a significant boost. Make sure to log in every day to claim your rewards. Participate in events to earn exclusive items and resources. These rewards can help you progress faster and become stronger.

Upgrading Outside the Game

Don't forget to upgrade your character outside of the main game. Use the resources you collect to enhance your abilities and equipment. This will make you stronger and better prepared for the challenges ahead. Keep an eye on the in-game store for special deals and items that can help you on your journey.
